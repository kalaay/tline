Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is tLine app 100% free or is there any in-app purchase?

    The basic use of the app is completley free but, the advanced features require a monthly subcription.

  • Can I use tLine from my phone?

    tLine is a responsive web application that can be used from any device including your phone and tablet.

  • How do I use WhatsApp on my computer?

    To provide you with a richer communication experience, tLine is accessible both on your phone and your computer. You can use it on your desktop without installing any software, simply log-in from any web browser. The projects you create or edit are fully synced between your phone and your computer, and you can see it all on both devices. Any action you take on the phone will apply to tLine on your computer and vice versa.
